Our Story

From a Family Kitchen to a Chocolate Home Factory

In 2013, Mahima began crafting small batches of chocolate truffles in the heart of our family home. Inspired by her grandmother’s recipes and guided by her parents’ dedication to quality, each piece was made with care, love, and carefully sourced ingredients.

Over the decades, what started as a personal passion evolved into a family-owned enterprise known for its artisanal approach, single-origin cocoa, and devotion to ethical sourcing. Today, Mariska's ChocoFactory represents the perfect blend of old-world tradition and innovative technique.

Sourcing & Sustainability

We believe that the best chocolates begin at the source. That’s why our cocoa beans are handpicked from smallholder farms from Idukki district in Kerala and Coimbatore in Tamil Nadu, where farmers are paid fair wages and supported with sustainable agricultural practices.

By forging close relationships with our suppliers, we ensure that every bean we roast carries a story of responsible stewardship, community empowerment, and environmental respect. Our packaging is eco-friendly, and we continuously seek new ways to reduce our environmental footprint, from bean to bar.

Meet the Team

Behind every box of Mariska's ChocoFactory is a group of dedicated individuals who share a passion for flavor, quality, and service. We are proud to introduce the family and team members who bring our vision to life every day.
