Mariska's ChocoFactory are now available for bulk or wholesale purchase, making it easy for specialty retailers, gourmet shops, and hospitality businesses to offer our handcrafted products. Our chocolates are made with ethically sourced cacao, ensuring exceptional taste and uncompromising quality.

Bulk Purchasing & Terms

Minimum Orders

We offer competitive pricing on minimum bulk orders starting at 20 boxes. This ensures you can maintain a steady inventory without oversaturating your shelf space.

Discount Structure

Enjoy tiered discounts based on order volume. The more you order, the more you save, allowing you to maximize your margins while delivering quality products to your customers.

Shipping & Lead Times

Our chocolates are made fresh to order. Lead times typically range from 1-2 weeks. We ship via climate-controlled carriers to ensure your chocolates arrive in perfect condition.
